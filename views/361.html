Vertical Black, Red, Blue László Moholy-Nagy

sculpture [source]

floating shape incised w/ circular overlapping lines on molded plexi, use of molded, transparent plexi - surface is not flat & it is see-through; red, black dots in lines creating radiating shapes, hard to tell where these are because of plexi they are on; also made kinetic works & used light. here both surface and placement of dotted lines on this surface create uncertainty

Details

  • medium: sculpture, oil & incised lines on molded Plexiglas, on original base
  • art movement: nan
  • year: 1945
  • size: 43 x 29 x 10 cm
