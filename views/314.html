Hername Hannah Wilke

print [source]

self-description using text, words like "epiphany," "chance" & "thank you" which include the letters "h," "a" & "n." She stacked 6 copies of each phrase, shifting them left & right, capitalizing the key letters so each vertical block spells "HANNAH". From distance this capitalization reads as a darker vertical line crossing at different points & each block reads as an arrow. Small size of letters in relation to paper & value are quiet but maybe not uncertainty though
