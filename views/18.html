Language is not transparent Mel Bochner

drawing [source]

angled repeated sentence; content plays a part in addition to overlapping, placement & the materials. Uncertainty is predominately through orientation of the sentences and their subsequent overlapping, without the varied orientation, this would not communicate a jumbled sense of sentences piled up on each other
