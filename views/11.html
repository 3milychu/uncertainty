Untitled Larry Bell

installation [source]

coating on glass greater at base, fragility of material, reflective properties, change in value on glass makes surface change from presence to almost not there. Value change on the glass from bottom to top changes the surface of the glass and makes it seem to somewhat float. If the glass didn't have this change in value, there would be much less a sense of uncertainty
